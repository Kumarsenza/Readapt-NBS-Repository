# ESPON ReAdapt NbS Knowledge Repository

**Re-Naturing Territorial Development for Climate Risk Adaptation**

🔗 **Live site:** https://kumarsenza.github.io/readapt-nbs-repository

---

## About

The ESPON ReAdapt NbS Knowledge Repository is an open-access interactive evidence base developed under the ESPON ReAdapt project, funded by the ESPON EGTC and project coordinated by Wageningen Environmental Research, Wageningen University & Research in close support Climate KIC . It brings together curated Knowledge base/output on Nature-based Solutions (NbS) for climate adaptation across European regions, making them searchable, filterable, and explorable through an interactive web interface.

---

## Knowledge Base
This project systematically maps the fragmented NbS knowledge landscape across four interlinked domains: (i) policy frameworks, (ii) EU funded implementation projects, (iii) knowledge sharing platforms and networks, and (iv) the scientific literature across EU Member States. 

Coverage: ESPON Member and Associated Countries, 30 European countries. Covers key climate hazards and includes a broad range of NbS solutions studied or implemented across these countries.

## Features

**🏠 Home — Explore & Search**
The landing page offers two ways to explore the evidence:

- **Search bar** — type any combination of keywords (topic, country, city, NbS type, hazard) and results appear instantly. Multi-word search scores and ranks results by relevance, with matched keywords highlighted. Click any result to open it directly.
- **Interactive filter map** — select a climate hazard (Step 1), territory type (Step 2), and governance scale (Step 3). The map updates in real time showing only locations where matching evidence exists. Multiple selections are supported — click any filter again to deselect it.

**🔍 Search & Browse**
Filter all 655 records across 9 dimensions — NbS type, hazard, territory, scale, implementation stage, community engagement, country, and year range — combined with free-text search. Expand any record to read the key lesson, barriers, and enablers.

**📊 Evidence Analytics**
Summary charts and statistics across the full knowledge base, including breakdowns by NbS type, climate hazard, territory, and country coverage across ESPON member states.

**🗺️ Evidence Map**
Full OpenStreetMap with all 355 geolocated records. Filter by NbS type, hazard, and record type. Click any pin to see the title and key lesson.

**🌿 NbS Taxonomy**
Structured overview of the five primary NbS types — Green Infrastructure, Blue Infrastructure, Ecosystem-based Adaptation, Hybrid/Engineered NbS, and NbS General — with definitions and sub-typologies.

**🧭 Decision Support**
Practical modules for spatial planners and local authorities:
- NbS Finder — match solutions to your hazard, territory, and governance context
- Comparable Cases — find implemented case studies similar to your situation
- Business Case — effectiveness evidence and co-benefits by NbS type
- Implementation Pathway — step-by-step guidance for planning and delivery
- Funding Navigator — relevant EU and national funding instruments
- Policy Alignment — links to EU policy frameworks and requirements
- Governance Requirements — lead actors, coordination mechanisms, and best practice

## How to Use

1. Open the live site at https://kumarsenza.github.io/readapt-nbs-repository
2. **Search** by typing keywords in the search bar on the home page — results appear instantly
3. **Or filter** using the three steps on the home page to explore the map by hazard, territory, and scale
4. Use **Search & Browse** for detailed filtering across all 655 records
5. Use **Decision Support** modules for practical planning guidance tailored to your context

The repository works in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile — no installation required.
